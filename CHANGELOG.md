# Changelog

## 1.0.0 (20 July 2026)

First complete release.

- Onboarding: household size, allergies, food preferences, rushed nights, and a plain-language consent screen (general information, never medical advice; data stays on device).
- Weekly planner: 27 whole-food, Mediterranean-pattern recipes (including Romanian home cooking); variety scoring across fish, legumes, vegetarian and batch meals; rushed nights get quick dinners or planned leftovers; swap any day; the planner learns loved and vetoed meals.
- Groceries: aggregated list by supermarket section, pantry-staple flags, your own extras, print view.
- Track: four gentle habit dots (moved, slept well, ate the pattern, water first), weekly check-in ritual that opens with what held, one active nudge at a time.
- Progress: energy, sleep and mood sparklines from check-ins, plus your own words. No weight anywhere, by design.
- Learn: 15 evidence cards with named sources and honest strength labels (strong, emerging, thin).
- Care: private appointments calendar, question list for the doctor, visit notes, optional PIN.
- Data: JSON backup and restore, full erase, no network calls of any kind.
- PWA: installable, offline after first load, light and dark modes, responsive from phone to desktop.
